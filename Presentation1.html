Practical Questions to Address

  1. Is the gender correlated to diabetes?

  2. Is age correlated to diabetes? If age will be a possible chance to cause diabetes such as if the person is older, will tat cause a higher chance to have diabetes?

  3. Is history of heart disease correlated to diabetes?

  4. Is smoking history correlated to diabetes?

  5. Is it possible that higher BMI, the higher chance to have diabetes?

  6. Is blood glucose level correlated to diabetes?
